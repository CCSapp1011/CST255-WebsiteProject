Welcome to my website! My name is Camryn and this is the main home page.

This site will be based around my favorite game:

My favorite game for the longest time has been Team Fortress 2, developed by Valve.

The simplicity and lighthearted feel of the game makes it feel very welcoming and re-playable with the amount of experiences you can have against other players. I've personally played over the span of a decade as it's a decently old game.

I've spent so much time fascinated with the game and its engine that I know many of the quirks and ways the game can react to certain instances of events in the game.

That said I've also played a few other games by Valve as many of them use the same engine, and have a similar gameplay feel.

I highly recommend you check it out!
